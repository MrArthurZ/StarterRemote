
# 🚀 StarterRemote

> **StarterRemote is a lightning-fast, automated job board curating the best entry-level and remote-first opportunities from around the web. Built for zero-friction job hunting.**

Finding your first remote job shouldn't require 5 years of experience. **StarterRemote** is a curated, automated job board designed specifically for beginners, career changers, and juniors looking to break into the remote work ecosystem. 

We aggregate top-tier, entry-level remote opportunities so you don't have to scroll through thousands of irrelevant listings.

## ✨ Features

* **🌍 100% Remote:** Every job listed is fully remote. No hybrid compromises.
* **🌱 Entry-Level Focus:** Curated specifically for Junior roles and people with little to no prior experience.
* **⚡ Lightning Fast:** Deployed globally via Cloudflare Pages for instant load times and optimal SEO.
* **🤖 Automated Curation:** Job listings are dynamically sourced and aggregated from trusted APIs (like Jobicy and Arbeitnow) using automated workflows.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

* **Hosting & CDN:** [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/) (Fast, secure, and globally distributed)
* **Job Sourcing:** Integrations with Jobicy, Arbeitnow, and other remote job APIs.
* **Automation:** Automated data fetching and content distribution via [n8n](https://n8n.io/).
